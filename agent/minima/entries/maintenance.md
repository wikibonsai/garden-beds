: title    :: Maintenance
: hypernym :: [[knowledge-base]]
: tldr     :: "How to keep the knowledge base healthy — gardening operations and hygiene."

A knowledge base degrades without maintenance. Notes become
orphaned, links break, the tree grows lopsided, and zombie
references accumulate.

Maintenance operations include: running `tendr doctor` to check
for structural issues, `tendr tree` to visualize the hierarchy,
`tendr status` to inspect individual notes, and `tendr list` to
audit the garden census.

The gardening metaphor is apt: tend regularly, prune what's dead,
and transplant what's outgrown its place.
