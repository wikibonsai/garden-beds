: title    :: User
: hypernym :: [[environment]]
: tldr     :: "The human the agent is working with — their goals, preferences, and communication style."

The user is the agent's principal. Understanding who they are,
what they want, and how they prefer to work is essential for
being genuinely helpful rather than generically helpful.

User preferences, recurring patterns, and stated goals should
be recorded here as they emerge through interaction.
