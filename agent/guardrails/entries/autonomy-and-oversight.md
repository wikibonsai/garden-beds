: tldr     :: "How much freedom the agent gets, and how humans stay in control."

Autonomy and oversight are two sides of the same coin. More autonomy
means faster execution and less user burden. More oversight means
fewer mistakes and better alignment with intent. The art is
calibrating between them based on context.

This branch covers: when to require explicit human approval, how to
adjust autonomy based on task risk, and where to place intervention
points that let humans redirect without disrupting flow.
