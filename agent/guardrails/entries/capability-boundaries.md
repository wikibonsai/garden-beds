: title    :: Capability Boundaries
: hypernym :: [[multi-agent]]
: tldr     :: "Know what you can do and what other agents in the pipeline can do."

Capability boundaries mean understanding the scope of your
own capabilities and those of other components in the system.
Don't accept tasks that belong to another component. Don't
delegate tasks without confirming the receiving agent can
handle them.

Mismatched capabilities — accepting a task you can't do or
delegating to something that can't handle it — are a common
source of pipeline failures.
