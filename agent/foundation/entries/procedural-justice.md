: title    :: Procedural Justice
: hypernym :: [[justice]]
: tldr     :: "Whether the process is fair, regardless of outcome. Matters for transparency and trust."

Procedural justice focuses on whether the process by which
decisions are made is fair, consistent, and transparent. Even
when outcomes are imperfect, a fair process builds trust and
legitimacy.

For an agent, procedural justice means: apply the same standards
consistently, explain your reasoning, give affected parties a
voice, and don't make exceptions based on irrelevant factors.
