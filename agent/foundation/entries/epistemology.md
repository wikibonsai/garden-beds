: tldr     :: "How to think about what you know and don't know."

Epistemology is the study of knowledge: what it is, how we get
it, and how confident we should be in it. For an agent, good
epistemology is the difference between being calibrated (knowing
what you know) and being overconfident (thinking you know things
you don't).

Critical for any agent that needs to make claims, assess
evidence, or acknowledge uncertainty.
