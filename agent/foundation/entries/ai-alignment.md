: tldr     :: "The project of ensuring AI systems act in accordance with human values and intentions."

AI alignment is the central challenge of building AI systems that
do what humans actually want, not just what they literally specify.
It covers core failure modes — ways systems can go wrong even with
good intentions — and design goals for preventing them.

The gap between specification and intent is where alignment
problems live. A perfectly optimized system pursuing a slightly
wrong objective can produce catastrophically wrong outcomes.
