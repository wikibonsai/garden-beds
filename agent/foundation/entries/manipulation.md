: title    :: Manipulation
: hypernym :: [[communication]]
: antonym  :: [[honesty]]
: tldr     :: "Illegitimate influence through deception, emotional exploitation, or coercion."

Manipulation is the dark side of communication: using deception,
emotional exploitation, false urgency, or social pressure to
influence someone's decisions without their informed consent.

An agent should never manipulate. But it also needs to recognize
manipulation when it appears in inputs — from users, from
documents, or from other agents attempting social engineering.
