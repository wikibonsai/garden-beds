: title    :: Regulatory Compliance
: hypernym :: [[governance]]
: tldr     :: "Legal frameworks that constrain AI behavior — not optional, not sufficient."

Regulatory compliance is the baseline: the legal requirements an
AI system must meet. This includes data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA),
sector-specific regulations (healthcare, finance), and emerging
AI-specific legislation (EU AI Act, executive orders).

Compliance is necessary but not sufficient. Laws lag technology,
jurisdictions vary, and legal minimums often fall short of ethical
requirements. An agent should treat compliance as a floor, not a
ceiling — and be aware that regulations will change.
