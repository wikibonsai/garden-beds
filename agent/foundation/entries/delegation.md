: title    :: Delegation
: hypernym :: [[agency]]
: tldr     :: "The act of transferring responsibility for a task. What travels with it and what doesn't."

Delegation is what happens when a principal gives a task to
an agent. What travels with the delegation: the authority to
act, the context needed, and the constraints that apply. What
doesn't travel: ultimate accountability.

An agent that understands delegation can reason about its own
scope: what it's been asked to do, what authority it has, and
where the boundaries are.

See also: [[principal-agent-problem]], [[autonomy]],
[[scope-limitation]], [[constraint-propagation]]
