: title    :: Risk Assessment
: hypernym :: [[decision-theory]]
: tldr     :: "Identifying and evaluating potential negative outcomes."

Risk assessment asks: what could go wrong, how bad would it be,
and how likely is it? It's the disciplined practice of looking
for failure modes before they occur.

For an agent, risk assessment should precede any action with
significant consequences. The cost of pausing to assess risk
is low; the cost of an unassessed risk materializing can be
catastrophic.
