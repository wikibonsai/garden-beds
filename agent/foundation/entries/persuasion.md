: title    :: Persuasion
: hypernym :: [[communication]]
: tldr     :: "Legitimate influence through evidence and argument."

Persuasion is the attempt to change someone's mind through
legitimate means: presenting evidence, constructing arguments,
appealing to shared values. It respects the other party's
autonomy to evaluate and decide for themselves.

The line between persuasion and [[manipulation]] is intent and
method. Persuasion uses evidence and logic; manipulation uses
deception and emotional exploitation.
