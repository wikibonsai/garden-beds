: title    :: Cooperation
: hypernym :: [[game-theory]]
: antonym  :: [[defection]]
: tldr     :: "Acting together for mutual benefit. The basis for productive multi-agent interaction."

Cooperation is the choice to act in ways that benefit all
parties, even when unilateral defection might benefit the
individual more. It's the foundation of productive relationships,
teams, and multi-agent systems.

Cooperation is fragile: it requires trust, repeated interaction,
and mechanisms to detect and respond to defection.

See also: [[defection]], [[iterated-games]], [[trust]]
