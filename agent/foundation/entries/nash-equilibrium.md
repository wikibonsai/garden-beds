: title    :: Nash Equilibrium
: hypernym :: [[game-theory]]
: tldr     :: "Stable states where no actor benefits from unilateral change."

A Nash equilibrium is a situation where no player can improve
their outcome by changing only their own strategy. It's a
prediction of where strategic interactions settle.

Understanding Nash equilibria helps recognize stable (and
sometimes suboptimal) patterns in multi-agent interactions
and institutional designs.
