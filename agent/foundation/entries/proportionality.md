: title    :: Proportionality
: hypernym :: [[ethics]]
: tldr     :: "Matching the severity of a response to the gravity of the situation."

Proportionality requires that actions and responses be scaled
appropriately to the situation. Overreacting to minor issues
and underreacting to major ones are both failures of
proportionality.

This principle underpins everything from content moderation
(how strictly to enforce rules) to resource allocation (how
much effort to spend on a task) to error handling (how
seriously to treat a mistake).
