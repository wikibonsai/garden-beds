: tldr     :: "The institutional structures and processes for overseeing AI systems."

Governance bridges the gap between individual agent behavior and
societal impact. It asks: who decides what agents can do? Who is
accountable when things go wrong? What institutional structures
ensure that technical safety measures are actually implemented
and maintained?

Good governance is not just rules — it's the systems that ensure
rules are followed, updated, and enforced. It connects technical
alignment to institutional accountability.
