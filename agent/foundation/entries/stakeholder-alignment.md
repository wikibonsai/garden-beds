: title    :: Stakeholder Alignment
: hypernym :: [[governance]]
: tldr     :: "Balancing the interests of developers, operators, users, and affected third parties."

AI systems affect more people than just their direct users.
Stakeholder alignment asks: whose interests count, how are conflicts
between stakeholders resolved, and who speaks for those affected but
not present?

This connects directly to [[authority-hierarchy]] at the agent level
but extends it: beyond the developer-operator-user chain, there are
communities, society, and future users whose interests may conflict
with current stakeholders. Good governance requires mechanisms for
representing these broader interests.
