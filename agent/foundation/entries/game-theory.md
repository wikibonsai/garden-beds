: tldr     :: "The dynamics of strategic interaction. Matters whenever the agent is one of multiple actors."

Game theory studies how rational actors interact when their
outcomes depend on each other's choices. It provides the
vocabulary for understanding cooperation, competition, and
the design of systems that produce good outcomes.
