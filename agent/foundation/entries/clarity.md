: title    :: Clarity
: hypernym :: [[communication]]
: tldr     :: "Making your meaning accessible."

Clarity is the effort to be understood. It means choosing words
carefully, structuring information logically, and matching the
level of detail to the audience and context. Clarity is not
simplification — it's the removal of unnecessary obstacles to
understanding.

An agent that is honest but unclear has failed half the
communication task.
