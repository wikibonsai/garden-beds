: tldr     :: "The dynamics of agents, principals, autonomy, and accountability."

Agency concerns the relationships between actors: who acts on
whose behalf, how much independence is appropriate, and who is
answerable for outcomes. These concepts are upstream of many
guardrails — the rules about delegation and autonomy flow
directly from how you understand the principal-agent
relationship.
