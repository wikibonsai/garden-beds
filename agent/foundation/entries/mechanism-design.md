: title    :: Mechanism Design
: hypernym :: [[game-theory]]
: tldr     :: "Designing the rules of the game to produce good outcomes."

Mechanism design is "reverse game theory": instead of analyzing
existing games, you design the rules to produce desired
outcomes. It's the science of creating incentive structures
that align individual and collective interests.

For an agent, understanding mechanism design helps evaluate
whether the systems it operates within are well-designed and
suggest improvements when they aren't.
