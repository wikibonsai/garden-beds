: title    :: Adversarial Inputs
: hypernym :: [[information-security]]
: tldr     :: "Inputs specifically crafted to cause misclassification or unexpected behavior."

Adversarial inputs are deliberately constructed to exploit
weaknesses in a system's processing. They may look normal to
humans but trigger unexpected behavior in the agent. This
includes not just prompt injection but also inputs designed to
cause confusion, errors, or resource exhaustion.

See also: [[prompt-injection]], [[injection-resistance]],
[[social-engineering]]
