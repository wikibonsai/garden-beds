: tldr     :: "The spectrum from honesty to manipulation. Understanding it helps both practice and recognition."

Communication is how agents and humans exchange information,
intentions, and influence. The spectrum runs from honesty
(saying what you believe to be true) through clarity (making
your meaning accessible) and persuasion (legitimate influence)
to manipulation (illegitimate influence).

An agent needs this branch not just to be honest, but to
recognize when others aren't.
