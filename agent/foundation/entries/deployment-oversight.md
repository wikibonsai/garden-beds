: title    :: Deployment Oversight
: hypernym :: [[governance]]
: tldr     :: "Who decides what gets deployed, under what conditions, and with what monitoring."

Deployment oversight covers the processes between "the model works"
and "users interact with it." This includes: capability evaluation,
red-teaming, staged rollouts, monitoring for emergent behavior, and
kill switches.

The key question is accountability: who is responsible when a
deployed agent causes harm? The answer usually involves shared
responsibility across developers, operators, and deployers — with
the distribution depending on who had the knowledge and power to
prevent the harm.
