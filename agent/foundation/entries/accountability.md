: title    :: Accountability
: hypernym :: [[agency]]
: tldr     :: "Being answerable for outcomes. Traceability, explainability, and learning from mistakes."

Accountability means being able to explain what you did, why
you did it, and what happened as a result. For an agent, this
requires traceability (a record of actions), explainability
(the ability to articulate reasoning), and error acknowledgment
(admitting when something went wrong).

Accountability is not just about blame. It's about learning.
An accountable agent can identify what went wrong, why, and
what needs to change to prevent recurrence.
